Yummy Pizza Recipe

Homemade Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, bell peppers, mushrooms, etc.)

    • Instructions:

  • Dissolve sugar in warm water, sprinkle yeast, and let it sit for 5-10 minutes until frothy.
  • Mix flour, salt, and olive oil in a bowl, then add the yeast mixture. Knead into a dough and let it rise for an hour.
  • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C). Roll out the dough, spread pizza sauce, add toppings, and sprinkle cheese.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  • Slice and serve!

    Nasi Goreng Recipe

    Yummey Nasi Goreng Recipe

    Ingredients:

  • 3 cups cooked white rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g chicken or shrimp, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, bell peppers)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
    • Instructions:
  • Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  • Add chicken/shrimp and cook until browned.
  • Push the ingredients to the side, crack eggs into the pan, scramble, and mix with the rest.
  • Add rice, soy sauce, chili sauce, and mixed vegetables. Stir-fry until heated through.
  • Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot!

    Kottu Roti Recipe

    Ingredients:

  • 2 cups chopped roti (flatbread)
  • 200g chicken or vegetables, finely chopped
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green chili, sliced
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

    • Instructions:

  • Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic, onion, and green chili until aromatic.
  • Add chicken/vegetables and stir-fry until cooked.
  • Push the ingredients to one side, crack eggs into the pan, scramble, and mix with the rest.
  • Add chopped roti, soy sauce, chili sauce, cabbage, and carrots. Stir-fry for a few minutes.
  • Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot!

    Chicken Biryani

    Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 4 tomatoes, chopped

    • Instructions:

  • Wash the rice and soak it for 30 minutes.
  • Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger, garlic, and spices for an hour.
  • Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden brown, add tomatoes, and cook until soft.
  • Layer the rice and chicken mixture in a pot, and cook on low heat until rice is done.
  • Garnish with fried onions, mint leaves, and serve hot.
